5.Make Dryflower

How To Make Dryflower?

  1. wash the stems of a flower
  2. Trim the leaves of a flower and cut off the stems a little.
  3. tie up the stem of a flower in a string.
  4. Hang the flowers upside down in the shade that doesn't shine.

When you receive flowers from an acquaintance, you can make and store a dryflower and keep it for the first time. I like flowers and I like to decorate my room, so whenever I get flowers as a gift, I always make a dry-flower to decorate my room. It's hard to make a dryflower and it's been a lot of failures, but I'm so happy when it's done beautifully.
